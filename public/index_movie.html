The Best Movies According to Tim

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Remember the Titans

This is my favorite movie of all. A mix of brotherhood and camaraderie, centered around unity in sports.

The Dark Knight

The greatest single acting performance I've ever seen with Heath Ledger and the Joker.

Happy Gilmore

I can watch this over and over again!

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Here are some main reasons why:

1. I love Snape in this movie.
2. I love the innocence, yet complexity of this movie.
3. I love Dumbledore - he's the best Dumbledore.
